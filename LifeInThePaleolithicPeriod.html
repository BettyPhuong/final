Life in
The Paleolithic Period

Project: Uni-project
Tool: Abobe Illustrator, Figma
Description:
‘LIFE IN THE PALEOLITHIC PERIOD’ is the topic I chose for creating the series. First, I was creating eight icons related to the Paleolithic period. I was then presenting it on digital posters and prototyping interfaces on a computer/ smartphone. The style I followed is Pixel art. I wanted to create a gaming story. Therefore when exploring this project, you play this game to unlock 8 ‘tools' in the Paleolithic period. It is one of my favorite projects because this is the first branding project. From now, I decided to deep-learning of interaction design.
